 calcview (pos, ang, fov, znear, zfar)
Called when the engine wants to calculate the player's view

Parameters:

  • pos: Current position of the camera
  • ang: Current angles of the camera
  • fov: Current fov of the camera
  • znear: Current near plane of the camera
  • zfar: Current far plane of the camera

Return value:

    table Table containing information for the camera. {origin=camera origin, angles=camera angles, fov=camera fov, znear=znear, zfar=zfar, drawviewer=drawviewer}
